Waffles

Some of the most delectible waffles you have ever seen.

These waffles can be done a little soft, or with a nice crunch to them.

A little vanilla adds just the perfect flavor on every waffle.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat waffle iron accrding to manufacturer's instructions.
  2. Whisk eggs in a large bowl until light and fluffy.
  3. Add flour,milk,and vegetable oil and mix to combine, then whisk in sugar,baking soda,salt,and vanilla until smook (careful not to overmix)
  4. Spray waffle iron with cooking spray, pour batter onto the hot waffle iron and cook until golden brown, around 3-5 min.
  5. Let cool and place onto serving plate.
