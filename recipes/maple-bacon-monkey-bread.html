Maple Bacon Monkey Bread

A delicious monkey bread glazed with a sweet maple syrup and bacon.

Quick homemade monkey bread done in and hour and a half.

A seven ingredient, seven step recipe for anyone to make.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 (12 ounce) package bacon
  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 3/4 cup packed dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 (16.3 ounce) packages refrigerated biscuit dough, separated and cut into quarters

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Coat inside of pan with cooking spray.
  2. Cook bacon in skillet over medium-high heat until evenly browned.
  3. Melt margarine in saucepan over medium heat; stir in brown sugar and maply syrup while bringing it to a boil. Cook for about 1 min then remove from heat.
  4. Mix whtie sugar and cinnamon in a resealable plastic bag. Add 6-8 biscuit pieces at a time to the bag and shake until coated. Place sugared biscuits onta a plate and repeat to coat rest.
  5. Any extra cinnasugar mix gets mixed with the maple syrup and heated over medium heat until sugar is dissolved.
  6. In the prepared pan, make four layers with bacon, maple syrup mixture, and biscuits. Finish with a drizzle of the maple syrup on top.
  7. Bake in preheated oven untill biscuits are cooked through, for about 35 min. Allow to cool for 10-20 min then place on serving plate.
