Mango Ice Cream

A creamy 3 ingredient mango ice cream.

Not only are these quick to make, but they are pretty easy too with only 3 ingredients.

With only an 8 hour wait in the freezer, this is such a great recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4-5 Mangos
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup white sugar

Steps

  1. Peel and dice mangos, then blend until pureed and set aside.
  2. Whip heavy cream and sugar with an electric mixer on high speed until soft peaks form. Reduce speed to low and mix in mango puree.
  3. Pour into freezer safe bowl and place in freezer for 8 hours.
  4. Once done let sit for 8-10 min to soften and enjoy.
